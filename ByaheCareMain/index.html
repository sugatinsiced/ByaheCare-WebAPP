Welcome to ByaheCare

ByaheCare is a web app designed to improve your commuting experience in the Philippines. It allows users to report and rate public utility vehicles (PUVs), such as jeeps, buses, and other vehicles. Whether you are looking to provide feedback, report issues, or simply share your experience, ByaheCare is here to help you.


How to Use ByaheCare:

  • Rate a Ride: Share your experience by rating the vehicle on a scale of 1 to 5 stars.
  • Report a Ride: If you've encountered a problem, you can submit a report with details such as the vehicle's plate number, type, and the issue.
  • View Complaints: Browse previous complaints submitted by you the user.


To get started, simply select one of the options in the navigation and begin contributing to the improvement of public transportation!
